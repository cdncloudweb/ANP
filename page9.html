A Legacy of Precision

Nakul's career began in the rigorous environments of the Big Four consulting firms. It was here that he mastered the art of financial forecasting, strategic risk assessment, and global operational standards. However, he noticed a gap: the same high-level strategic intelligence was often out of reach for agile, fast-scaling enterprises that needed it most.

ANP Consulting was founded on the principle that precision shouldn't be a luxury. By blending the structured discipline of traditional consulting with modern, data-driven analytics, Nakul created a firm that bridges the gap between ambitious vision and executable reality.
